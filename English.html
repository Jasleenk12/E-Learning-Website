English courses cover language, literature, and communication skills. Students study grammar, vocabulary, and language structure, enhancing their proficiency in written and verbal communication. They analyze literary works from different genres and time periods, gaining insights into storytelling, symbolism, and cultural contexts. English education fosters critical thinking, analytical skills, and the ability to articulate ideas effectively. Students also explore the impact of literature on society and its reflection of historical and societal issues. Additionally, English courses often address digital communication and media literacy, preparing students to engage with diverse forms of written and visual information in a global context.
